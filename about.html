About me

Front-end developer from Johannesburg, South Africa

I possess a diverse educational background and hold expertise across multiple fields. My professional journey began as a receptionist for a funeral investment services firm, where I gained invaluable experience in administrative duties and client relations over the course of one year.

Driven by a passion for healthcare, I pursued further education at Wits University and subsequently worked in the medical field for five years, in school of nursing. Transitioning into the corporate world, I served as a secretary at a private electrical contractor company for three years.

Inspired to broaden my horizons, I pursued a National Diploma in Civil Engineering, expanding my knowledge base and skill set. Throughout my journey, I have remained committed to lifelong learning, continually seeking new opportunities to expand my skills and knowledge.

I believe in the power of continuous growth. I am always eager to embrace challenges and explore new avenues for personal and professional development.
